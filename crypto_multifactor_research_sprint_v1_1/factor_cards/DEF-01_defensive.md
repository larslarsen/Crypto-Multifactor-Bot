# Factor Card DEF-01 — Defensive Risk

**Status:** Preregistered  
**Family:** Risk/quality  
**Primary horizon:** 7 days  
**Expected sign:** Lower risk receives a higher score

## Canonical characteristics

- negative 30-day realized volatility;
- negative 30-day downside semivariance;
- negative 60-day market beta magnitude;
- negative 60-day idiosyncratic volatility.

Market beta and idiosyncratic volatility use rolling regressions estimated only from lagged data.

## Primary score

Average the standardized realized-volatility and downside-risk scores. Beta and idiosyncratic volatility are confirmatory extensions after coverage is verified.

## Role

This factor is tested both as:

1. a return predictor;
2. a portfolio risk-control characteristic.

Those results are reported separately.

## Reject as alpha when

It improves Sharpe only by lowering beta/volatility but has no positive alpha or incremental net return. It may still remain a risk-control input.
