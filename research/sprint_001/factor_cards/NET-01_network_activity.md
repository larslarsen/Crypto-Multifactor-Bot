# Factor Card NET-01 — Network Activity and Protocol Economics

**Status:** Phase 2 / working-paper motivated  
**Family:** Fundamentals and adoption  
**Primary horizon:** 7 to 30 days

## Candidate characteristics

- active-address growth;
- transaction-value growth;
- fee/revenue yield;
- retained protocol revenue relative to market value;
- supply or staking participation measures;
- network adoption changes.

## Preconditions

- asset-to-chain/protocol taxonomy;
- publication-time reconstruction;
- revision policy;
- no cross-chain double counting;
- no use of current protocol classification in historical periods.

## Baseline test

Each metric is tested individually before any composite. A price-only baseline and size/liquidity controls are mandatory.

## Evidence status

Published evidence supports the importance of network adoption broadly; several specific cash-flow and staking signals remain working-paper hypotheses and require independent replication.

## Reject when

The result depends on revised historical data, current classifications, or a handful of large protocols.
