# External Scientific Review Triage

## Classification

The owner relayed external reports dated 2026-07-20. They are advisory, identify no audited
repository commit, and provide no independently resolvable primary-source citations. They may
motivate review but are not empirical evidence in the Evidence Registry.

## Accepted

- Sprint 002 momentum requirements need canonical time-series operationalization and explicit
  experiment registrations.
- Inference configuration should identify the estimand, resampling unit, block/lag rule,
  cluster counts, multiplicity family, and economic threshold before execution.
- Funding-state reporting and objective structural-break definitions can extend the existing
  robustness matrix.
- DIL-01 and NET-01 entry decisions should use measured coverage, lag, revision, mapping, and
  vintage-integrity fields.
- A recurring literature refresh is useful.

## Accepted with modification

- Capacity reporting uses preregistered target capital multiples and break-even capacity,
  not a universal $1m/$10m/$50m/$100m grid.
- Effective sample size is reported only when estimator-defined. Every experiment instead
  reports decision dates, independent rebalances, asset/date clusters, and bootstrap blocks.
- Regime splits use lagged, preregistered state variables. Retrospective labels such as
  "regulatory era" or the ex-post worst 20% are descriptive only and cannot support promotion.
- A minimum economically material effect must be frozen per experiment; positivity alone is
  not rebranded as economic significance.

## Deferred

- Residual momentum variants beyond existing MOM-01 are challengers until audited size and
  control data exist.
- Fixed AUM grids may be added as presentation cells after an empirical impact model and
  target capital are approved.
- Romano-Wolf may replace or supplement Holm only after its implementation is validated and
  the confirmatory family is preregistered.

## Rejected

- Replacing the once-per-version sealed holdout with rolling opened six-month windows.
- Editing Sprint 001 factor cards or IDs in place.
- Mandatory size controls before point-in-time supply data pass audit.
- Selecting a "top three" factor baseline from observed performance.
- Treating all inference estimators as interchangeable confirmations.
- Making residual momentum plus funding carry the first experiment. Standalone attribution
  and the baseline ladder come first.
