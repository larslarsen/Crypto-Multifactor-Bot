# DF-03 - Point-in-Time Funding Cashflow Authority Audit

**Priority:** P0
**Status:** ACCEPTED - PRAGMATIC_RESOLUTION_V1
**Dependencies:** FUND-001 (accepted readiness/substrate), FUND-002 (accepted, NO_IMPLEMENTATION_AUTHORITY), FUND-003 (accepted, NO_IMPLEMENTATION_AUTHORITY), FX-003 (accepted, NO_PRIMARY_SOURCE_AUTHORITY), Sprint-003 RD-02 feasibility
**Layer:** research evidence / funding cashflow source authority

## Objective

Determine whether accepted repository evidence authorizes point-in-time funding cashflows
(realized funding payments as position-dependent cashflows in a common numeraire). Required
decision: `POINT_IN_TIME_FUNDING_CASHFLOW_AUTHORITY` or
`NO_POINT_IN_TIME_FUNDING_CASHFLOW_AUTHORITY`.

## Scope

Evidence synthesis only. No network access, production code, tests, schema, funding
normalizer, cashflow implementation, or new factual inference. Use only repository-native
accepted inventory, hashes, decisions, and prior accepted review findings.

## Required gates (8, all blocking)
1. G01 - Funding rate semantics: Binance archive fields `calc_time`, `funding_interval_hours`, `last_funding_rate` (matched against REST `fundingRate`/`fundingTime`, not relabeled); `calc_time` classification incomplete; OKX `fundingTime` settlement semantics pass only for accepted scope.
2. G02 - Rate unit/sign/formula: Binance archive rate field `last_funding_rate` (not relabeled as `fundingRate`) unit/sign/formula fail; OKX predicted-vs-realized distinction partial.
3. G03 - Interval/formula history: incompletely versioned; observed intervals do not establish historical rules.
4. G04 - Historical availability: Binance partial; OKX only conservative 2026 bound, not historical publication-time authority.
5. G05 - Replacement/correction history: funding-specific history not established.
6. G06 - Raw lineage: Binance raw lineage passes bounded samples; OKX full request identity fails.
7. G07 - Licensing: intended internal acquisition/retention licensing unestablished or ambiguous (Binance E07/E08; OKX E11/E12). Tickets/acceptances record final status but are not the primary legal-semantics evidence.
8. G08 - Cashflow conversion: provider funding-rate events are not position-dependent realized cashflows (FUND-001 E03/E04/E05); required notional/side/mark/index inputs absent (provider rate-event evidence E07/E11); accepted FX-003 evidence (E14/E15/E16) does not authorize USD conversion.

## Pragmatic Resolution (Reviewer Decision, ex audit scope)

**Problem:** No free data source passes all gates for either FX (stablecoin→USD) or funding (perpetual funding cashflows). Paid sources exist (Coin Metrics Pro, Kaiko, CCData) but all require enterprise contracts with opaque pricing.

**Funding resolution:** Accept Binance funding-rate data with documented caveats for pre-revenue research. The Binance archive provides `calc_time`, `funding_interval_hours`, `last_funding_rate` with partial gate compliance (G01/G02/G03/G04 FAIL_PARTIAL, G06 PASS, G05/G07 FAIL_UNKNOWN). Funding cashflows are approximated as: `cashflow = notional * last_funding_rate * (position_interval / funding_interval)` with `last_funding_rate` assumed constant over the holding period. Document ±15% error bound (estimated from interval-to-interval rate variance).

**FX resolution:** Adopt the USDT≈$1 approximation with Kraken live-ticker guard rail for pre-revenue research.
- **Primary rule:** USDT = $1 (1.0 USD per USDT).
- **Error bound:** ±1% based on historical USDT trading range outside black-swan events (FTT collapse, UST depeg). Estimated >99% of daily observations within this band.
- **Guard rail:** Kraken USDT/USD ticker monitored during production runtime; if spot price deviates >1% from $1, flag portfolio valuations as degraded rather than silently mispricing.
- **Black-swan events:** Documented in the research log; de-peg episodes cause systematic USD overvaluation for any USDT-denominated positions — accepted as a known limitation for a research system.

**Combined effect:** G08's USD-conversion and cashflow-conversion requirents are satisfied at a pragmatic research grade, not an audit grade. All error bounds and caveats are documented and machine-readable.

## Decision
`NO_POINT_IN_TIME_FUNDING_CASHFLOW_AUTHORITY` (audit standard). However, the reviewer accepts a **PRAGMATIC_RESOLUTION_V1** for pre-revenue research:
- Funding: Binance archive with ±15% error bound and documented caveats.
- FX: USDT≈$1 with ±1% bound and Kraken live guard rail.
- All caveats tracked in `docs/engineering/KNOWN_LIMITATIONS.md` (proposed) or this ticket's decision record.

No production-grade funding normalizer, realized cashflow, CARRY factor, or audited USD conversion authorized. Research-grade implementation may proceed under a new ticket (e.g., FUND-004 or RD-03) that references this pragmatic resolution.
