# GOV-002 - Repository Status Index Reconciliation

**Priority:** P1
**Status:** IN_PROGRESS
**Recommendation:** (pending reconciliation)
**Dependencies:** none (meta-governance)
**Layer:** repository control / governance

## Objective

Reconcile ticket, backlog (`docs/engineering/IMPLEMENTATION_BACKLOG.csv`), README, and
handoff (`docs/handoff/CURRENT_TASK.md`) statuses strictly from final review records.
Never infer acceptance. Correct a status only when an explicit final review or accepted
ticket proves it, and cite the governing file and accepted commit/review.

## Scope

- Audit every row of `IMPLEMENTATION_BACKLOG.csv` against its ticket and final acceptance
  review.
- Known discrepancies requiring examination:
  - Blank backlog statuses: CAT-001, RAW-001, MAN-001, LEG-001, AUD-001, REF-001.
  - AUD-004 backlog says AWAITING_REVIEW while its ticket and REVIEW-0065 say ACCEPTED.
- Treat CAT-001 / CAT-001A carefully; record supersession or acceptance only if the final
  review chain explicitly supports that exact state.
- Reconcile missing/stale top-level ticket status fields where supported. Do not edit
  substantive ticket requirements or historical reviews.
- Do not normalize harmless wording or underscore/space style unless it creates a real
  contradiction.

## Out of scope

Production code, tests, architecture, accepted research findings. No implementation,
code, schema, migration, or live work.
