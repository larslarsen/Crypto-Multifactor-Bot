**Priority:** P0

**Layer:** research evidence / dilution-unlock source authority

## Objective

Determine whether accepted repository evidence authorizes point-in-time token-unlock
schedules (historical unlock-schedule vintages, announcement/revision known-time, and actual
on-chain unlock execution). Required decision: `POINT_IN_TIME_UNLOCK_AUTHORITY` or
`NO_POINT_IN_TIME_UNLOCK_AUTHORITY`.

## Scope

Evidence synthesis only. No network access, production code, tests, schema, factor work,
procurement, or new factual inference. Use only repository-native accepted inventory, hashes,
decisions, and prior accepted review findings.

## Required gates (8, all blocking)
1. G01 - Access: Tokenomist TLS-unreachable in audited environment; Messari requires account/key; DefiLlama emissions HTTP 402. Not universal unreachability.
2. G02 - Vintages: historical unlock-schedule vintage preservation unproven.
3. G03 - Timing: announcement/revision publication-known-time history unproven.
4. G04 - Execution: actual on-chain unlock execution not queried or reconciled.
5. G05 - Mapping: token/contract/chain mapping across schedule and execution evidence unproven.
6. G06 - Coverage: some adapter artifacts exist, but representative token/asset output coverage not demonstrated.
7. G07 - Licensing: licensing and internal raw-retention authority unproven.
8. G08 - Reconstruction test: required known-unlock test did not reconcile announcement, revision history, and actual execution.

## Decision
`NO_POINT_IN_TIME_UNLOCK_AUTHORITY`. All eight gates are blocking (G01 FAIL_ACCESS;
G02/G03/G04/G05/G07/G08 FAIL_UNKNOWN; G06 FAIL_PARTIAL). Tokenomist DEFERRED, Messari
CONDITIONAL/EXPLORATORY_PHASE2, DefiLlama unlock adapters CONDITIONAL/REFERENCE_METADATA.
Current schedules/adapter code are not historical vintages. DIL-01 remains DEFERRED/UNTESTED;
DF-01 supply blocker independent and unchanged. No unlock collector, factor, schema,
implementation, procurement, or next ticket authorized.
