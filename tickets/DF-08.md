# DF-08 - Survivorship-Free Universe Source Authority Audit

**Priority:** P0
**Status:** ACCEPTED - NO_SURVIVORSHIP_FREE_UNIVERSE_AUTHORITY
**Dependencies:** REF-001 (accepted substrate), REF-002 (accepted, NO AUTHORITY), REF-003 (accepted, NO_AUTHORITY), Sprint-003 launch/scheduled-delivery metadata and bounded trade observations
**Layer:** research evidence / universe source authority

## Objective

Determine whether accepted repository evidence authorizes construction of a
survivorship-free historical universe (complete listing/delisting events, final tradable
price, and failure cause per asset). Required decision:
`SURVIVORSHIP_FREE_UNIVERSE_AUTHORITY` or `NO_SURVIVORSHIP_FREE_UNIVERSE_AUTHORITY`.

## Scope

Evidence synthesis only. No network access, production code, tests, schema, universe
implementation, or new factual inference. Use only repository-native accepted inventory,
hashes, decisions, and prior accepted review findings.

## Required gates (8)
1. G01 - Substrate: REF-001 accepted bitemporal identity/event-storage substrate only.
2. G02 - Event authority: launch/scheduled-delivery metadata (Bybit launchTime; BTCUSDU26 scheduled future deliveryTime, not an observed completed delivery) and bounded trade observations do not establish complete event authority.
3. G03 - Trade edges: bounded earliest/latest timestamps within sampled/archive objects are not proven asset-lifetime first/last trades and must not be equated with exact listing/delisting events.
4. G04 - Timing: announcement known-time and effective-time history unproven.
5. G05 - State transitions: historical state-transition and revision/vintage history unproven.
6. G06 - Delisted coverage: representative delisted/failed-asset coverage, final tradable price, and failure cause not demonstrated.
7. G07 - Licensing: required source licensing and internal raw-retention authority not established.
8. G08 - Reconstruction test: DF-08 required known-delisting reconstruction test not passed.

## Decision
`NO_SURVIVORSHIP_FREE_UNIVERSE_AUTHORITY`. G01 PASS (non-blocking); G02/G03 PASS_PARTIAL and
G04/G05/G06/G07/G08 FAIL are all blocking. REF-001 substrate and market-bar authority
preserved; REF-002/REF-003 accepted decisions block Bybit historical/prospective authority;
no accepted cross-venue source closes the gaps. REF-001 substrate and BAR-001 accepted
canonical-bar authority (E15/E16) are preserved and not downgraded. Historical universe
construction and dependent factor work remain blocked.

## Stop Condition
Publish the authority report and return control to Reviewer with
`Next ticket authorized: NONE`. Historical universe construction and all dependent factor
work remain blocked. Accepted market-bar and REF-001 substrate authority are preserved.
