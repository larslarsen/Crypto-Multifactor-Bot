**Priority:** P0  
**Dependencies:** CAT-001, data audit findings  
**Layer:** reference

## Objective

Represent assets, venue instruments, aliases, listings, contract terms, migrations, and known-time semantics independently of ticker strings.

## Deliverables

- stable asset/instrument/venue IDs;
- bitemporal records with valid-time and system-known-time;

- manual resolution queue for ambiguity;
- no automatic merge based only on symbol text;
- synthetic tests for ticker reuse, redenomination, migration, delisting, and late metadata correction.
